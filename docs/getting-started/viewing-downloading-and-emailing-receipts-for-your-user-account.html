查看，下载和通过电子邮件发送您的npm用户帐户的收据¶

注解

This article only applies to users of the public npm registry.

You can view, download, and email receipts for the complete billing history of your npm user account.

查看收据¶

  1. 使用您的用户帐户登录npm。 npm登录对话框，用户名和密码字段留空

  2. In the upper right corner of the page, click your profile picture, then click Billing Info. profile dropdown menu with billing information element selected

  3. On the Billing Information page, under “monthly bill”, click View Billing History. view billing history button

  4. To view a single receipt, find the row of the receipt you want to view, then, on the right side of the row, click the view icon. view receipt icon

下载收据¶

  1. 使用您的用户帐户登录npm。 npm登录对话框，用户名和密码字段留空

  2. In the upper right corner of the page, click your profile picture, then click Billing Info. profile dropdown menu with billing information element selected

  3. On the Billing Information page, under “monthly bill”, click View Billing History. view billing history button

  4. To download a single receipt, find the row of the receipt you want to download, then, on the right side of the row, click the PDF icon. download pdf icon

  5. To download all receipts, next to “Date”, select the checkbox, then click Download Checked. download checked receipts link

通过电子邮件发送收据¶

  1. 使用您的用户帐户登录npm。 npm登录对话框，用户名和密码字段留空

  2. In the upper right corner of the page, click your profile picture, then click Billing Info. profile dropdown menu with billing information element selected

  3. On the Billing Information page, under “monthly bill”, click View Billing History.

  4. To download a single receipt, find the row of the receipt you want to download, then, on the right side of the row, click the email icon. email receipt icon

  5. To email all receipts, next to “Date”, select the checkbox, then click Email Checked. email checked receipts link

  6. In the Email Receipt dialog box, fill in the “From”, “To”, and “Message” fields. email receipt form with from, to, and message fields

  7. 单击发送。

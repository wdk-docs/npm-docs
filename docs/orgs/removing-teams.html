删除团队¶

As an Org owner or team admin, you can remove teams that no longer need access to a set of packages governed by your Org. Removing the team will not remove the team members or packages from your Organization.

Log in to npm with your user account. npm login dialog with username and password fields left blank In the upper right corner of the page, click your profile picture, then click Profile Settings. npm avatar menu with selector over profile settings list item In the left sidebar, click the name of your Org. example org selected in organizations list On the Org settings page, click Teams. teams tab Beside the name of the team you want to remove, click X. x button
